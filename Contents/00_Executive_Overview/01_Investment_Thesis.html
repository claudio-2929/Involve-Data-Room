03
The Structural Market Gap
Today's EO ecosystem is fragmented — no vertically integrated Earth Intelligence infrastructure exists
There is currently no vertically integrated, persistent, AI-native Earth Intelligence infrastructure combining a controlled sensing layer, an autonomous optimization layer, and an intelligence orchestration layer. Involve fills this structural gap.
🛰️

Satellites

Periodic coverage, limited persistence. Cannot loiter or dynamically retask over a target area.

🚁

Drone Systems

Localized footprint, operationally constrained. No persistent regional coverage at altitude.

📦

Data Vendors

Sell imagery without integrated intelligence. No vertical control of the sensing layer.

📊

Analytics Platforms

Rely on third-party data they do not control. No sensing-layer ownership.

Involve's Answer — Three Integrated Layers
L1

Controlled Sensing Layer

Stratostats and Stratorelay platforms provide persistent, taskable regional coverage at lower revisit cost than satellites.

Hardware
L2

Autonomous Optimization Layer

Neurostar flight control enables autonomous navigation, mission optimization, and fleet-level coordination.

AI / Software
L3

Intelligence Orchestration Layer

CLEAR GeoAI transforms multi-source data into actionable intelligence via vision-language models and agent orchestration.

Platform
04
Involve's Integrated Architecture
Three compounding technological layers, each reinforcing the others
🎈

Stratospheric Platforms

Stratostats, Stratorelay, and the LoonHive roadmap provide persistent regional coverage, lower revisit cost versus satellites, taskable missions, and modular payload integration. Persistence increases data frequency and economic value per monitored asset.

Hardware Layer
🧭

Neurostar — Autonomous Flight Control

Enables autonomous navigation, mission optimization, energy-efficient persistence, and fleet-level coordination logic. Creates defensibility at the mobility and control layer.

Control Layer
✨

CLEAR — GeoAI Infrastructure Layer

Transforms multi-source geospatial data into actionable intelligence through vision-language models, structured geospatial databases, tool-based agent orchestration, and automated time-series analysis.

Intelligence Layer
Compounding Effect

Each layer reinforces the others. More flights generate richer training data, improving Neurostar efficiency, enabling lower-cost CLEAR data provision, and compounding the intelligence and margin advantage over time. CLEAR enables clients to interact with Earth data as a decision system rather than as raw imagery.
